HostileNPC

Superclass: NPC
Declared In: HostileNPC.h

Discussion

NPC subclass representing hostile NPC. In addition to interactions mechanism, it adds method attack that would kill either player or NPC depending on whos power is higher.


Methods

-attack

attack

- (BOOL) attack;
Return Value

YES if attack was successful and NPC was killed. NO, if player died.

Discussion

Attacks NPC. If its power is less than player's, NPC dies and player is given carriedItems. Otherwise player is killed.
